Welcome

American Yawp MS is a free, open-source American history textbook for middle school students (grades 6–8). It's adapted from The American Yawp, the collaboratively built college textbook used by hundreds of thousands of students nationwide.

This project treats students like thinkers, not test-takers. Every chapter features storytelling, primary sources, multiple perspectives, and the voices history left out. Read more about the project →

Volume II: Since 1877

The Gilded Age through the 21st Century (Chapters 16–30) — Planned for future development

Volume II is planned for later development. Most middle school curricula end with Reconstruction, and most high schools start American history at or after this point. If there's demand for a middle school adaptation of Volume II, we'll build it.
