Under Construction

The Primary Source Reader is in development. It will include historical documents, images, and artifacts for each chapter—carefully selected and annotated for middle school readers.

Check back soon, or contribute to the project on GitHub.

What's Coming

The Primary Source Reader will feature:

  • Historical documents — Letters, speeches, laws, and declarations
  • Visual sources — Paintings, photographs, political cartoons, and maps
  • Material culture — Artifacts, architecture, and objects from the past
  • Student-friendly annotations — Context, vocabulary, and guiding questions
  • Alignment with chapters — Sources organized to match the textbook's structure

The goal is to give students direct access to the voices and evidence of the past, helping them practice the skills of historical thinking: sourcing, contextualization, corroboration, and close reading.
